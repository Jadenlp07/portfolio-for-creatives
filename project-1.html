Shared Standard Mobile app ☏

Shared Standard is an app designed to help roommates create and maintain a shared cleaning standard for their apartment or house. Instead of dealing with unclear expectations or household tension, roommates can agree on cleanliness levels, responsibilities, and routines together in one place. By setting shared expectations and gentle reminders, the app helps reduce stress, prevent conflicts, and keep everyone happy while living together.

Building Shared Standard

IXD 105, 2026

The building process for Shared Standard started by identifying a common issue between roommates stress and conflict caused by unclear cleaning expectations and household responsibilities. Working alongside Ian and Aurod, we discussed ideas and asked key questions about how roommates communicate standards and what would make coordination easier and more fair. These conversations helped shape the main goal of the app: creating a shared space where everyone can agree on expectations together instead of relying on assumptions or reminders from one person.

Building Shared Standard

IXD 105, 2026

After defining the concept, we designed the app in Figma, creating wireframes and prototypes to visualize how users would navigate and interact with the features. We tested the designs by asking users to complete tasks and share feedback about usability and clarity. Through this process of questioning, testing, and refining, we improved the interface to make it simple, collaborative, and less stressful for roommates to maintain shared living standards.

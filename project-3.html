Shipyards Nightmarket web

The Shipyards Night Market Web redesign is a project focused on improving the digital experience of a popular local event. The goal was to create a more engaging and easy-to-navigate website that highlights vendors, events, and the overall atmosphere of the market. The redesign emphasizes bold visuals, clear information hierarchy, and a modern layout to better reflect the energy of the night market. By prioritizing user experience and accessibility, the site allows visitors to quickly explore schedules, discover vendors, and plan their visit with ease across both desktop and mobile devices.

Shipyards Re-Design Process

IXD 107, 2026

The redesign process for the Shipyards Night Market website began by identifying key issues with the original site. I focused on problems such as unclear navigation, lack of visual hierarchy, and difficulty finding important information like vendors, schedules, and event details. From there, I moved into sketching initial ideas on paper to quickly explore layout options and user flow. These rough sketches helped me experiment with different structures and prioritize what content should stand out most to users visiting the site.

Shipyards re-design process

IXD 107, 2026

After refining the direction, I created more detailed mockups in Figma to establish the visual style, layout, and overall user experience. This stage allowed me to define typography, color choices, and interactive elements while ensuring consistency across the design. Once the mockups were finalized, I built the actual website, translating the design into a functional and responsive experience. Throughout the process, I focused on creating a clean, modern interface that improves usability and better represents the energy and atmosphere of the Shipyards Night Market.
